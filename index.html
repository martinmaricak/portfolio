Perks from my experience:

5 years ago, I've started my design career as a web designer, preparing visual designs and developing frontend of wordpress themes along with ait-themes.club team. In my first year, lot of my time was spent in figuring out a rocket surgery of killing an evil nuances lurking in every corner of vast backward compatible stylesheets. My designs were featured at industry influencing websites like Awwwards honorable mention or Webdesignerdepot article. As a next step, I've joined a dream team at startup called Goldee. We were creating a hardware product and underlying platform that was aspiring to be Nest for intelligent lightning. I've designed blog, an internal web application and Goldee application for Android. After Goldee failed on it's journey, I've decided to try, how it feels to work in a corporation. After a few weeks, I've became a part of an international team providing offshore development for a big player in sports media streaming industry. While maintaining and enhancing multilingual sport media web portal, I've also participated in development of Quatar 2015 handball championship website. After finishing my master's degree, I went freelance. Now I'm taking challenges in small up to medium sized projects, mostly from enterpreneurs, or nice companies.

Education:

In 2015 I've graduated with master's degree in computer science. My graduation thesis was trying to answer to a question: How we can integrate touch gestures into integrated development enviroments and make them beneficial for the users. I was part of a team of three students whitch conducted user research, testing and implemented a lightweight IDE for mobile devices running Android.
